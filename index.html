Frédéric Bouchard

I am an experimental physicist working in the field of quantum photonics.

I am a scientist at the National Research Council Canada, where my research explores ultrafast optical methods for generating, controlling, and measuring quantum states of light.

Research Publications CV

Research focus

My work sits at the intersection of ultrafast optics, nonlinear photonics, and quantum information science.

Ultrafast quantum photonics

Quantum information encoded in short optical pulses and picosecond-scale time bins.

Nonlinear optics

Frequency conversion, Kerr interactions, squeezing, and photon-number-state manipulation.

Quantum communication

Telecom-compatible quantum states and protocols for high-rate photonic quantum technologies.
