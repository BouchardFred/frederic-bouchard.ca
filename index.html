Research themes

My work combines ultrafast optics, quantum information, and nonlinear photonics to build high-speed quantum technologies compatible with telecom wavelengths.

Ultrafast temporal modes

Encoding, manipulating, and measuring quantum information in picosecond-scale time bins and optical temporal modes.

Nonlinear quantum optics

Using χ² and χ³ nonlinearities for frequency conversion, squeezing, photon-number-state manipulation, and quantum measurement.

Photonic quantum processing

Developing scalable architectures for high-rate photonic quantum circuits, measurement, and feed-forward in temporal modes.
