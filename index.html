Research overview

My work combines ultrafast optics, nonlinear photonics, and quantum information science. I develop experimental platforms for quantum communication and photonic quantum processing, with a strong emphasis on telecom wavelengths and ultrafast temporal encoding.

Current themes

Ultrafast time-bin encoding, nonlinear quantum optics at telecom wavelengths, programmable photonic circuits, and high-speed measurement of quantum states of light.
