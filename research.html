Research

My research focuses on ultrafast quantum photonics: using short optical pulses, temporal modes, and nonlinear optics to process quantum information at high rates.

Ultrafast photonic quantum circuits

Programmable optical circuits based on temporal encoding, enabling compact and passively stable approaches to quantum information processing.

Telecom nonlinear quantum optics

Quantum frequency translation, Kerr switching, squeezing, and photon-number-state manipulation in fiber-compatible wavelength bands.

Quantum measurement

High-speed measurement schemes including photon-number-resolving detection, homodyne-style measurements, and ultrafast tomography.

Quantum networks and interconnects

Photonic components and protocols that could support high-rate quantum communication and quantum networking.
